# Phase 3: Advanced Topics

This phase explores advanced Zig programming concepts and techniques.

## Chapters

6. **Data Encoding and Processing** - CSV, JSON, XML, and database interactions
7. **Functions** - Zig's unique features: `defer`, `errdefer`, and explicit allocators
8. **Structs, Unions, and Objects** - Data-oriented design, tagged unions, and composition
9. **Metaprogramming** - The power of `comptime`
10. **Modules and Build System** - `build.zig`, packages, and imports

## What You'll Learn

These chapters go beyond the basics, showing you how to leverage Zig's compile-time capabilities and build sophisticated programs.
